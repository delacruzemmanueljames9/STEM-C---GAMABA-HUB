📜 Literature & Epic Chanters

Ginaw Bilog (Mindoro) Isinusulat ang Amahan poem sa kawayan. Binubuo ito ng 7 syllables bawat linya. Awarded ng panahon ni Fidel Ramos.
Fernando Caballero (2000) Epic chanter. Pinakatanyag ang Humadapnon, ang longest epic na tumatagal ng 162 hours.

🎶 Music & Performance

Masino Intaray (1993 - Pres. Ramos) Palawan master. Basal (Gong ensemble sa ritual), Tambilaw (Rice sharing ritual), Kulilal (Lyrical singing), Bagit (Mimics nature), Aruding (Mouth harp), at Babarak (Ring flute - nose/mouth).
Uwang Ahadas (2000) Yakan musician. Kilala sa Kwintangan Kayu (wood) at Agung (metal gong).
Samaon Sulaiman (1993) T'boli master ng Kudyapi/Kutyapi (2-string guitar na mukhang bangka).
Alonzo Saclag (2000) Kalinga musician/dancer. Founder ng Kalinga Budong.

🧶 Master Weavers

Lang Dulay (1998 - Pres. Estrada) Dream Weaver (T'boli). Gumagamit ng Abaca fiber. Designs: Buling Langit (clouds), Bankiring (bangs), Kabangi (butterfly).
Salinta Monon (1998) Bagobo weaver ng Inabal fiber. Sikat sa Binuwaya (crocodile) design.
Darhata Sawabi (2004 - Pres. Gloria) Tausug master ng Pis Syabit weaving.
Magdalena Gamayo Ilocano weaver ng Abel (cotton). Patterns: Spiral at Sinan-sabong (flowers).
Ambalang Ausalin Yakan master ng Tennum (cloth weaving). Patterns: Sinaluan (small diamond) at Siputangan (geometric).
Estelita Bantilan (2016) B'laan weaver. Gumagamit ng Pandanus/Pandan. (Bi = Opponent, Laan = People).
Yabing Masalon Dulo (2016) Ikat weaver. Mabal (process) at Tabih (finished product).

🔨 Crafts & Metal

Eduardo Mutuc (2004) Kapampangan metalsmith (Apalit). Secular art para sa simbahan.
Teofilo Garcia (2012) Abra. Gumagawa ng Tabungaw headpiece mula sa Upo gourd.
